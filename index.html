How Wira Works

Design
Model
Execute

Wira bridges the gap between IT and business, hence empowering your people to get the results they want, when they want them.

1.Design your business Processes

Wira provides a visual design environment that enables you map out your business processes,with little or no technical knowledge. Processes designed can then be uploaded to wira in the administration page

  • Invoice Approval(Pt1) A sample Invoice approval process
  • Invoice Approval(Pt2) Second part of the approval process
  • Processes Upload Designed processes are uploaded via the administration page

2. Model data needed for the process

With a drag and drop form Builder, Wira allows you to build your organisational forms - representation of data needed at every stage of your process. The forms designed are linked to the process through the administration page.

  • Powerful Form BuilderScreenshot of the Powerful Form Builder
  • Form PropertiesEasily change and customize Form Properties

3.Execute your process and easily evolve

Once you are done with the first two steps, you are ready to roll. You can now start executing your business process and it would work as provided in your defination. Alerts, reports and monitoring are also facilitated within the system
